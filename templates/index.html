What do we do

No Indian sleeps hungry

A food tracker where people donating food can book their slot for the particular area they are trying serve such that the other people who are willing to donate can have a track of the people donating and contact them to cover the entire locality. This will prevent multiple food packet distribution to a person at a time and also areas which are where no one is distributing food can be tracked by the government or any other individual can be serviced. This will help each and every need person get his meal.
